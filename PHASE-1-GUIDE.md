## üìä NEW FEATURES

### 1. Engagement Funnel Metrics ‚≠ê

**New Inputs:**
- **Impressions** - How many times your ad was shown
- **Clicks** - Number of clicks on your ad  
- **Orders** - Number of orders/conversions

**New Metrics Calculated:**
- **CTR (Click-Through Rate)** = Clicks / Impressions
  - Shows creative & targeting effectiveness
  - Industry benchmark: 2-5% is good
  
- **CPC (Cost Per Click)** = Ad Spend / Clicks
  - Your average cost per click
  - Compare to CPC goals and benchmarks
  
- **CPM (Cost Per 1K Impressions)** = (Ad Spend / Impressions) √ó 1000
  - Cost to reach 1,000 shoppers
  - Standard metric for brand awareness
  
- **Click-to-Order Rate** = Orders / Clicks
  - Conversion rate from click to purchase
  - Shows listing quality & price competitiveness
  
- **CPO (Cost Per Order)** = Ad Spend / Orders
  - How much you pay in ads per order
  - Key efficiency metric
  
- **AOV (Average Order Value)** = Sales / Orders
  - Average dollar value per order
  - Higher AOV = better unit economics
  
- **Units Per Order** = Units / Orders
  - Average basket size
  - Shows if driving bulk purchases

**Visual Elements:**
- **Funnel Visualization** - See conversion from Impressions ‚Üí Clicks ‚Üí Orders
- **Metrics Grid** - All engagement metrics at a glance

### 2. Customer Acquisition Analysis ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê

**New Inputs:**
- **NTB %** (New to Brand %) - % of sales from new customers
- **New Customers** - Count of unique new customers acquired

**New Metrics Calculated:**
- **NTB Sales ($)** = Total Sales √ó NTB %
  - Dollar value from new customers
  - Proves you're driving trial
  
- **Repeat Sales ($)** = Total Sales - NTB Sales
  - Dollar value from existing customers
  - Shows repeat purchase value
  
- **Repeat Customer %** = 100% - NTB %
  - Percentage from loyal customers
  
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)** = Ad Spend √ó NTB %
  - Total investment in acquiring new customers
  - Compare to customer lifetime value
  
- **CAC Per Customer** = CAC / New Customers
  - Average cost to acquire each new customer
  - Critical for ROI calculations

**Visual Elements:**
- **NTB vs Repeat Split Chart** - Visual breakdown of sales mix
- **Automatic Insights** - AI-powered recommendations based on NTB%

## üéì PRO TIPS

### Optimize Engagement Funnel
1. **CTR < 2%?** ‚Üí Test new creative, headlines, images
2. **CPC > $3?** ‚Üí Refine targeting, improve Quality Score
3. **Conversion < 4%?** ‚Üí Optimize listing (images, copy, price, reviews)
4. **CPO > $75?** ‚Üí Issue is upstream (CTR or conversion)

### Maximize Customer Acquisition
1. **NTB < 20%?** ‚Üí Expand targeting for trial
2. **NTB > 50%?** ‚Üí Consider retention campaigns
3. **CAC > $20?** ‚Üí May be too expensive unless high LTV
4. **Repeat < 40%?** ‚Üí Focus on product quality & satisfaction

### Balance the Mix
- **Too much NTB:** Risk acquiring unprofitable customers
- **Too much Repeat:** Missing growth opportunities
- **Sweet spot:** 25-40% NTB for growth with profitability
